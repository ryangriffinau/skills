What is the single smartest — most radically innovative, accretive, useful, and
compelling — addition you could make to the plan (or, if work is already underway,
the project) at this point?

$ARGUMENTS

Hold a high bar:

- Reach for the highest-leverage, most compounding move — not incremental polish.
- Name the one unlock we're a step away from but haven't surfaced.
- Then give 2–3 strong runner-ups, ranked by impact-to-effort.

For the top pick, say why it's the highest-leverage move and the first concrete
step to start it.
